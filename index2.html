About KCG

What is KEKW Circle Game?

KEKW Circle Game (KCG) is an exciting event where players compete in various games selected randomly by our famous Wheel of Fortune. Each spin determines the next challenge, keeping participants on their toes!

Founded in 2023, KCG has quickly become the most thrilling gaming event in the community. With substantial cash prizes and an electrifying atmosphere, it's more than just games – it's an experience you won't forget.
